How to play

  • Shoot Bubbles: Aim and shoot bubbles of the same color to create matches of three or more.

  • Use Boosters: Collect and use special power-ups to clear difficult levels.

  • Complete Levels: Reach the objectives of each level to progress.

  • Collect Stars: Earn stars by completing levels with fewer shots.

  • Unlock New Challenges: Progress through the game to discover new levels and obstacles.
